Strobe rate sets the lock frequency of the inner ring. The disk internally rotates at half this rate. Outer rings (4, 8, 16, 32, 64, 128, 256 segment-pairs) lock at 2× through 128× the strobe rate. 7 rings hard-coded; 256 integration samples per frame.

Pitch reference: A4 = 440 Hz, equal temperament. The LED fires only at peak amplitude (top 10% of signal range), giving crisp lock indication.

Picking an audio source (microphone or tab audio) replaces the synthetic sine with real-time samples; for tab audio, choose a tab in the share dialog and check “Share tab audio”.
