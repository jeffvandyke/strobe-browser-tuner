Strobe rate sets the lock frequency of the inner ring (which has 2 segment-pairs — B/W/B/W). The disk internally rotates at half this rate. Outer rings (4, 8, 16 pairs) lock at 2×, 4×, 8× the strobe rate. Picking an audio source (microphone or tab audio) replaces the synthetic sine with real-time samples; for tab audio, choose a tab in the share dialog and check “Share tab audio”.
